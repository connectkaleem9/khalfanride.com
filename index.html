About Us

YOUR TRUSTED PARTNER
ACROSS THE HOLY CITIES

KhalfanRide was founded with one mission: to make travel across Saudi Arabia's holy cities safe, comfortable, and stress-free for every pilgrim and traveler. From Jeddah Airport to the gates of Makkah and Madinah, we have served over 1,000 families with zero compromise on quality.

Our professional drivers are vetted, experienced, and speak English and Urdu. Every vehicle in our fleet is clean, well-maintained, and insured. We operate 24/7 — including late-night pickups, early Fajr transfers, and peak Hajj season — with fixed prices and no hidden fees.
